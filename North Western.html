    • Munneshwaram Hindu temple


      • Open 5.30 a.m. -8.00 p.m.
      Munneswaram hindu kovil is one of the important pancha ishwara hindu kovil in Sri lLanka in chilaw of puttalam district western province of Sri Lanka.
      This regional hindu temple complex has been in existence at least since 1000CE as myths surrounded with the temple associate with the Indian epic Ramayana .



    • World's Tallest Granite Samadhi Buddha statue



      • It was situated in north western province in Sri Lanka and this vihara was build in the rambodagala area near ridigama in Kurunegala.
      The Samadhi statue is 67.5ft high and it is made of huge granite rock which is currently the tallest statue in Sri Lanka.
