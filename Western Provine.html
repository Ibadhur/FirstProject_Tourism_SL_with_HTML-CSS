    • Thudugala Ella

      • Open 24 hours
      This waterfall located in thudugala village at kaluthara district there are many species of birds. These include the migrant bird “Yellow Magta”, with its colorful plumage and yellow underbelly and dicaeum erythornynchos the smallest bird in Sri Lanka the route to the waterfall strarts from katukararunda junction the galle road; 5 miles 8.0 km from there on matugama road to thudugala junction and from there 8 miles 12.9 km o the waterfall.
