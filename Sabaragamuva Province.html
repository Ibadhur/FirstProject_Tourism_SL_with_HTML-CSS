      • It is in Sabaragamuwa district. Adam's peak is 2,243m (7,359ft) tall conical mountain located in central Sri Lanka. It is well known for the Sri Pada.







    • Udawalawe Trails


      • Opens 8.30 a.m. - 6.00 p.m.
      Located in south-central Sri Lanka, Udawalawe National Park is about 310 sq km (118 sq mi) in size and has a resident population of some 1000 wild Asian elephants, which roam freely across the park En route to wild coast lodge from Ceylon tea trails, stop midway at the Dilmah supported Elephant Transit home at Udawalawe National Park which cares for orphaned elephants.
