    • Yala National Park


      • Open 6.00 a.m. - 6.00 p.m.
      This a most visited and second largest national park in sri lanka this is situated in the south east region of the country and lies in Southern Province and Uva Province. Yala National Park covers 979square kilometers. It was designated as a wildlife sanctuary in 1900. The park is best known for its variety of wild animals and it is important for the conservation of Sri Lankan elephants, Sri Lankan leopards and aquatic birds.
