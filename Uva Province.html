    • Nine arch bridge


      • Opens 12.00 a.m. - 11.59 p.m.
      In 1921 the nine arch bridge stands. Proudly, it is ideally placed between the ella and demodara railway station, those choosing to walk along the bridge will be presented with scenes of rolling hills. His bridge is 300 feet in length 25 feet in width and 80-100 feet in height.







    • Kataragama Temple


      • Opens 9.30 a.m. - 11.30 p.m.
      This temple complex dedicated to Buddhist guardian deity katargama deviyo and hindu war god murugan. The shrines and the nearby kiri vehera are managed by Buddhists, the shrines dedicated to teyvanai and shiva are managed by hindus and mosque by muslims. The entire temple complex was declared a holy place by the government of sri lanka in the 1950s, since then political leaders have contributed for its maintenance.
